Sligo Animal Rescue

Helping Pets in Need Across Sligo

Animal Rescue and Rehabilitation

Our team works tirelessly to rescue stray, abandoned, and injured animals across Sligo. Each animal receives medical care and rehabilitation tailored to their needs.

We ensure that every rescued pet is nurtured back to health in a safe, loving environment before being made available for adoption.

Adoption Services

We help connect rescued animals with caring, responsible families through our adoption program. Each adoption includes guidance on pet care and ongoing support.

Prospective adopters can meet our animals in person, learn about their personalities, and find the perfect companion for their home.

Community Education and Support

Sligo Animal Rescue provides workshops, school visits, and community outreach to promote responsible pet ownership and animal welfare.

We also offer volunteer opportunities, fostering programs, and guidance for families seeking advice on caring for their pets.
