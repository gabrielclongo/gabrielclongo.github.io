Hook Head Lighthouse

Experience Ireland’s Ancient Beacon on the Coast of County Waterford

Guided Lighthouse Tours

Step inside one of the world’s oldest operational lighthouses and take a guided tour through its stone towers. Learn about the monks who first kept the beacon alight 800 years ago.

Knowledgeable guides will lead you through centuries of maritime history while you climb to the lantern room for sweeping views of the sea and countryside.

Coastal Walking Trails

The dramatic Hook Peninsula coastline offers a variety of scenic walking paths. Wander along rugged cliffs, sandy coves, and tidal pools while spotting seabirds and seals.

These trails are ideal for both leisurely strolls and more adventurous hikes, giving you the chance to breathe in fresh Atlantic air and enjoy breathtaking scenery.

Family-Friendly Visitor Centre

The visitor centre at Hook Head Lighthouse provides interactive exhibits, a café with local treats, and plenty of activities for children to enjoy.

Whether you’re stopping by for lunch or browsing the gift shop for local crafts, the centre makes a welcoming base for your day at the lighthouse.
