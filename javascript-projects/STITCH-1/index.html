The Foundation

Crafting Community in the Smoke & Iron.

Established in the historic fabric of Smithfield, Roast & Iron was born from a desire to blend Dublin's industrial grit with the warmth of artisanal hospitality.

We source our beans directly from single-estate farmers, roasting in small batches to preserve the unique narrative of every harvest. Our space is more than a café; it's an archive of the neighborhood's evolving spirit.
